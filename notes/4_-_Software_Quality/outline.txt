Context

What is Software Quality?
	* Meet specification
	* Usability, Testability, Safety, Security, Maintainability, Efficiency, Reliability, Portability
Why Important?
	* Cost of error correction
	* Can lead to useless systems
How to ensure?
	* Quality Management Process
		* Quality Assurance
			* Establish framework of procedure and standards
			* (Software/Product/Process) Standards
			* ISO Standards
		* Quality Planning
			* Select the procedures and standard to use
		* Quality Control
			* See that the picked procedures and standards are used correctly. 
			* Review and inspections
